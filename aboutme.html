About

Being born and raised in San Francisco has influenced me to think more about technology and its advancements. I am fortunate enough to live in a city where tech is thriving and going to continue to flourish over the course of many more years.
During my sophomore year, I decided to take on Girls Who Code. I began taking classes every Saturday from 10am-12pm at my local library to learn more about coding and computer science. When the classes ended, I felt myself expressing interest in this field. I decided to apply to the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program to spend my summer expanding my knowledge even more. I believe the summer program will equip me with many strong resources needed to continue my journey in exploring computer science.
